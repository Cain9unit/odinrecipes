Fall skillet hash

Description

This is a dish invented by Paul Saladino, author of the Carnivore Code cookbook. Butternut squas h, apple and thyme infuse a beef- and bacob hash and eggs with sweet and familiar flavors of fall. A perfect dish for a low carb diet.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of peeled butternet squash cubes
  • 1 tablespoon tallow or butter
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 600 grams of ground beef
  • 1 large appel, cut into small cubes
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. preheat the over to 200°C. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. in a large bowl, toss the squash with melted butter or tallow. Spread the squash on the baking sheet and put it into the oven.
  3. cook the bacon until brown and cut it into small pieces
  4. bake the ground beef in a big pan, stirring to break up the meat until brown.
  5. After removing the beef from the pan, add the apple cubes and bake them.
  6. Add the squash, bacon, thyme and salt to the pan. Reduce the heat.
  7. Make three indentations in the dish. Crack an egg into each of the indentations.
  8. Cook for 7 or 8 minutes and the dish will be done
